About Us

A student-led gateway to the cosmos, dedicated to unraveling the mysteries of the universe through observation and scientific inquiry.

The Astronomy & Astrophysics Society, GCOEA is a student-led initiative based at the Government College of Engineering Amravati, Maharashtra. The society operates under the Department of Physics with the support and guidance of Dr. Deepak A. Zatale, Head of the Department.

It was founded in February 2025 by Jayesh Rajesh Warghat, Durgesh Pramod Sapkal, Mehul J. Dhakne, and Om Rajesh Chandore with the vision of promoting interest and awareness in astronomy and astrophysics among students.

Unlocking Mysteries

The universe is vast, fascinating, and full of mysteries waiting to be explored. Our society was established to inspire curiosity about the cosmos and encourage students to learn about space science through discussions, educational activities, and observational experiences.

Remote Outreach

A key objective of the club is to reach students in remote areas who are interested in astronomy but lack proper facilities and guidance. Through outreach programs and knowledge-sharing initiatives, we aim to make astronomy accessible and inspire young minds to look up at the night sky with curiosity and scientific understanding.

Our Mission

The mission of the Astronomy & Astrophysics Society, GCOEA is to promote awareness and understanding of space science among students and the wider community. Through educational programs, discussions, and outreach activities, we aim to spark curiosity about the universe.

In addition to spreading knowledge, the society strongly believes in learning through practical experience. We provide students with hands-on opportunities to observe the night sky and learn how to operate different types of telescopes.

By guiding them in handling and using astronomical instruments properly, we help them gain real observational experience and develop a deeper connection with astronomy.
